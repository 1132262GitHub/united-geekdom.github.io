Independence special

They're isolated, what a thing!
They must be an estranged tribe to sing.
Not a tribal chant,
Which our culture does grant,
But their anthem's 'God Save the King'!

(Happy Indian Independence Day from United Geekdom . 72 years since we've had to sing this song!)
