Limericks


I am a huge fan of limericks. I write, frankly, terrible limericks, and am very glad to be sharing them with my audience here, tiny as said audience may be. Scroll down for today's limericks, and further still for the rest of them. A limerick will be added everyday.

The salmon, insured

Fred the salmon had his life insured
Though his friends thought insurance was fraud.
But when he was fished
and his breathing was wished
He was taken away, and cured!

The Ivor's

There once was a group called the Ivor's
In whose tale there are no survivors.
They drowned to their ends
with their brothers and friends
in a pool-they were synchronised divers!
