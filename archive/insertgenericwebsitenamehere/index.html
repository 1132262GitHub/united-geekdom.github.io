So I had actually created this website on the 27th of July 2019, one week before it was moved to GitHub. I just called it the generic website, and it was created using Serveo It was just created as my friend created their website, RandomWebsite (later to become RandomSite), also using Serveo. However, Serveo required an installation of a server, and the server was an absolute pain to maintain (hey, that rhymed!). I had to keep my computer running 24/7, or else my website wouldn't work. So one week later I made the switch to GitHub, as it seemed much easier to use and I was already familiar. A couple of days later, my friend made the same move for RandomSite. I was also not proud of the name which I had bestowed upon my website, so I dubbed my new website United Geekdom, which, for reasons unbeknownst to myself or anyone I know, I am much prouder of. This also, interestingly, features a poem which was later removed. Of course, in a couple of months the entire poems section would be removed and relocated to Pencil Publishers.
