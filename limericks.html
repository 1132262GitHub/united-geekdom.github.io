I am a huge fan of poetry. I write, frankly, terrible poems, and am very glad to be sharing them with my audience here, tiny as said audience may be. A poem will be added everyday. This page is for today's poem.

The salmon, insured

Fred the salmon had his life insured
Though his friends thought insurance was fraud.
But when he was fished
And his breathing was wished
He was taken away, and cured!
